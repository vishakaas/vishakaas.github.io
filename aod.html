Accessibility on Demand

I worked in a group of 6 to design a learning space on campus that accommodates up to 100 learners with diverse abilities. We represented Accessibility on Demand, a design firm, who implements accessible methods into new buildings to support the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act and drive Ontario to a barrier-free zone. The user design, ethical, political, cultural, and business impacts were thoroughly researched to reach the final design.

Our final RFP was selected for an ongoing accessible classroom plan within Ryerson's Centre for Excellence in Teaching and Learning.
