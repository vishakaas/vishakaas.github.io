Lactic Acid Fermentation

Objective: Research a product manufactured in the bioprocessing industry that uses enzymes or cells.

I worked in a team of 4 to complete the project through the following 4 phases:

  • Conducting preliminary research for a literature survey to determine the feasibility of the topic
  • Designing an e-Poster to summarize findings efficiently by utilizing the graphic tools included in Microsoft PowerPoint to create process diagrams and flowcharts
  • Preparing a final report on the background of lactic acid fermentation, relevant upstream process routes, downstream operations, industrial applications, benefits, and summary of findings.

    • Our group received 93% on the final project.
