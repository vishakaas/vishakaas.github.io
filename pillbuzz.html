Pill Buzz

Objective: Research and propose a new solution for a pre-existing issue in the baby boomer generation.

I worked in a group of 8 to redesign an average pill bottle and optimize its functionality by making it portable and convenient with installed audible (buzzer), visible (light) and other programmed features to notify the user when to take the pill.

The final design was implemented in a design report that included:

  • Bill of Materials
  • Evaluation
    • Durability
    • Sustainability
    • User Experience
    • Performance
    • Profitability
    • Affordability
    • Accessibility
    • Public Good
  • Redesign
    • Method
    • Cost
    • Portability
    • Range of Use
    • Simplicity
    • Power Consumption
    • Marketing

    Our group received 97% on the final project.
